1. Policy Overview

At Oropendola AI, we strive to provide the best AI-powered coding assistance service. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, this Refund Policy outlines when and how you can request a refund.

Our Commitment: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time Pro and Team subscriptions (conditions apply).

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Eligible Refund Scenarios

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • Service Unavailability: Service was unavailable for 48+ consecutive hours due to our infrastructure (not third-party AI providers)
  • Billing Errors: You were charged incorrectly or twice for the same subscription
  • First-Time Purchase: Within 7 days of your first Pro or Team subscription (see conditions below)
  • Unauthorized Charges: Your account was used without authorization (with proof)
  • Service Not as Described: Core features advertised are not functional

2.2 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

For first-time Pro or Team subscribers:

  • Request refund within 7 days of initial purchase
  • Applies only to first subscription (not renewals)
  • Must not have exceeded 50% of monthly usage quota
  • Account must not have violated our Terms of Service
  • Does not apply to annual subscriptions (prorated refunds only)

Example: If you purchased a Pro monthly plan on January 1st and are not satisfied, you can request a full refund by January 7th (provided you used less than 50% of your monthly quota).

2.3 Prorated Refunds

For annual subscriptions, we offer prorated refunds:

  • Available within 30 days of purchase
  • Refund = (Paid Amount) - (Months Used × Monthly Equivalent)
  • Subject to fair use evaluation
  • Not available if usage exceeded reasonable limits

4. Non-Refundable Items

4.1 No Refunds For

  • Renewal Charges: After 48 hours of auto-renewal (cancel before renewal to avoid charges)
  • Partial Months: Unused portion of current billing period (except annual plans)
  • Free Tier: No charges, no refunds
  • Excessive Usage: If you exceeded fair use limits before refund request
  • Terms Violations: If your account was terminated for violating Terms of Service
  • Third-Party Issues: Outages or issues with OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google AI services
  • Change of Mind: After 7-day window (for monthly) or 30-day window (for annual)
  • Downgrades: Downgrading to a lower tier does not qualify for refund

4.2 Usage-Based Restrictions

Refunds may be denied if:

  • You used more than 80% of your monthly quota
  • Your account shows abusive or automated usage patterns
  • You generated excessive API requests
  • You used the service commercially without a Corporate plan

5. Refund Process

5.1 How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Email us at hello@oropendola.ai or billing@oropendola.ai

Step 2: Include the following information:

  • Your account email address
  • Subscription plan name
  • Purchase date and transaction ID
  • Reason for refund request
  • Any supporting documentation (if applicable)

Step 3: Our billing team will review your request

  • Initial response within 48 hours (business days)
  • We may ask for additional information
  • Decision made within 5 business days

5.2 Refund Approval Criteria

We will evaluate:

  • Whether your request falls within eligible timeframes
  • Your account usage and activity
  • Whether Terms of Service were followed
  • Any technical issues you experienced
  • Previous refund history

5.3 Refund Decision

If approved:

  • You'll receive a confirmation email
  • Refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • Your subscription will be canceled (if active)
  • Access continues until the end of the current billing period

If denied:

  • You'll receive an explanation
  • You may appeal the decision within 7 days
  • Final decision will be made by senior management

7. Cancellations

7.1 Canceling Your Subscription

To avoid future charges:

  • Cancel at least 24 hours before your next billing date
  • Cancellations can be done from your Account Dashboard → Billing
  • You will receive a cancellation confirmation email
  • Access continues until the end of the current billing period

7.2 Cancellation vs. Refund

Important: Canceling your subscription does NOT automatically trigger a refund. If you want a refund, you must explicitly request it per the process above.

7.3 Auto-Renewal Accidents

If you forgot to cancel before renewal and were charged:

  • Request refund within 48 hours of charge
  • Must not have used the service during new billing period
  • Full refund will be issued
  • Subscription will be canceled

8. Chargebacks & Disputes

8.1 Contact Us First

Before filing a chargeback with your bank, please contact us. Chargebacks:

  • May result in immediate account suspension
  • Take longer to resolve than direct refund requests
  • May incur processing fees (which we may pass to you)
  • Can be avoided if you contact us first

8.2 Chargeback Consequences

If you file a chargeback:

  • Your account will be suspended pending investigation
  • We will provide evidence to your bank (transaction records, usage logs)
  • If chargeback is ruled in your favor, we will not dispute further
  • If ruled in our favor, your account will remain suspended until payment is resolved
  • Frivolous chargebacks may result in permanent account termination

8.3 Payment Disputes

For payment disputes (incorrect amount, unauthorized charge), contact us immediately at billing@oropendola.ai. We will investigate and resolve within 5 business days.
