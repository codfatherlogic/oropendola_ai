1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, enabling essential features, and analyzing how you use our service.

Cookies typically contain:

  • A unique identifier
  • The website domain name
  • Expiration date
  • Small amounts of data relevant to your visit

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in: Remember your login status across sessions
  • Remember your preferences: Store settings like language, theme, or dashboard layout
  • Analyze usage: Understand how you interact with our service to improve it
  • Ensure security: Protect against fraudulent activity and unauthorized access
  • Provide personalized experience: Customize content based on your usage patterns

5. Managing Cookies

5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

On your first visit, you'll see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize cookie preferences

5.2 Dashboard Settings

You can manage cookies from your account dashboard:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy
  3. Select Cookie Preferences
  4. Enable/disable cookie categories

5.3 Browser Settings

All modern browsers allow you to control cookies:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Warning: Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using core features of Oropendola AI.

5.4 Opt-Out of Analytics

To opt-out of Google Analytics:

  • Install Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Or disable analytics cookies in your Dashboard Settings

6. Cookie Consent

6.1 Your Choices

By using Oropendola AI, you can choose:

  • Accept All: Enable all cookies for the best experience
  • Essential Only: Enable only required cookies
  • Custom: Choose specific cookie categories

6.2 Consent Management

You can change your consent at any time:

  • Click the cookie icon (🍪) in the footer
  • Visit Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences
  • Clear your browser cookies and revisit the site

6.3 For EU Users

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Access your cookie data
  • Request deletion of cookie data
  • Object to cookie processing
